Rhythmic variations
===================

.. tech:technique:: rhythmicvariations
   :displayname: Rhythmic Variations
   :rating: 5

   Play a section with varying rhythms.  For straight 8ths or 16ths, play with dotted rhythms (slow-fast and fast-slow), and vice-versa.
